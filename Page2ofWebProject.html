The Career Of Rock Drummer Matt Sorum




Background Informastion On Matt Sorum

Matt sorum is one of the most hard hitting drummers in the industry. He has had a very lucrative career playing drums. He has been in some of the biggest bands in the world and also has has worked with some icon bands such as Motorhead,Deep purple and ZZ top. Matt got into the drums as a child he saw Ringo Starr playing drums on the Edd Sullivan shown and instanly fell in love with drums. His parents bought him his first drum kit when he was about 5 years old and started taking lessons. He has said over the years he puts his craft down to a lot of practice and lessons. In school he was fortunate enough to to he able to have music classes every day and would run home from school every day and practice his drums for about 3 hours every day.
